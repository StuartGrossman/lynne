How it works

Send me an email with the title : "Virtual Color Consultation"

We’ll then talk about the scope of your project and the direction you’re thinking for color. After you will email me picture's of the areas to be painted, indicating where the (North, South, East, West) natural light comes from.

Once we determine the color or colors to be used from the information you’ve given me. I will then format the colors and send you a template with the picture of your room/rooms with the colors next to the room showing placement.

What you need

A computer or Smart Phone

For Email or Skype purposes

A camera

High quality pictures of windows, flooring and any furnishings

A PayPal account

Not necessary but preferred

A Color Fan

Linke to buy either the Classic or Color-Preview deck (Color Preview has all the whites, Classic does not)
